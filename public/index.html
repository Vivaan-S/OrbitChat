Why Blabbr?

Fast speed

Our servers update every millisecond, granting our users fast speed. It takes about 1 millisecond for messages to appear on another user’s device.

Real-Time bolt

Our Node server runs 24/7 meaning messages are getting passed around whenever, wherever. You can always be in touch with your friends.

Secure lock

If you join a room, you can only see the messages sent after you joined, and we don’t save any of your conversations either, so you can talk with privacy.
